# Helpline Source Record

Last reviewed: June 21, 2026

Kaval treats helpline data as safety-critical. Numbers should be reviewed periodically and dial actions must continue to open the Android dialer rather than placing calls without user confirmation.

## Primary National Source

India.gov.in Helpline Directory:

```text
https://www.india.gov.in/directory/helpline
```

Confirmed from the directory during this review:

- 112: Integrated Emergency Response Support System
- 100: Police
- 101: Fire
- 102: National Ambulance Service
- 181: Women Helpline
- 1098: Child Helpline
- 1930: Cyber Crime
- 1070: Relief Commissioner for Natural Calamities
- 139: Railway security and medical assistance

## Additional National Entries

- 108 is labelled as an emergency ambulance service with a clear note that availability varies by state.
- 1091 is described narrowly as help for obscene, harassing, or threatening calls.
- 14416 is listed as Tele-MANAS mental-health support.

## Excluded Pending Verification

- 1078 was not used; the current India.gov.in directory lists 1070 for national calamity relief.
- Bengaluru Hoysala 103 was not added because a current official owner/source was not confirmed during this review.
- Vimochana was not added until its current number and service ownership can be confirmed directly.
