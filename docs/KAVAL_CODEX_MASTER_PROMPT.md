# Kaval Codex Master Prompt

Kaval is a native Android MVP for a personal safety companion app. It focuses on emergency assistance, trusted contacts, live safety status, fake call support, appearance customization, and a local-first demo experience.

The product positioning is gender-neutral: Kaval is a personal safety companion app for broad everyday safety scenarios.

Priority:

```text
Safety > Clarity > Speed > Reliability > Visual polish
```

Build a native Android app with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Material 3, MVVM, Navigation Compose, Room, and DataStore. The MVP must use local-first mock behavior only: no real SMS, phone calls, paid map APIs, backend, or AI risk prediction.
